IMPORTANCE OF EDUCATION

Education plays a vital role in personal and national development. It helps people gain knowledge, develop skills, and make informed decisions. Through education, individuals can improve their lives and contribute positively to society.

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."
- Nelson Mandela

This quote reminds us that education has the power to transform lives and create opportunities. Investing in education is one of the best ways to build a better future.
