My Daily Routine

My daily routine helps me stay organized and productive. I start my day early and follow a schedule that includes work, learning, and personal activities.

Morning Activities

I wake up at 4:30 a.m. and begin my day with prayer. After praying, I wash the utensils and sweep the compound. When I finish my chores, I take a shower, get dressed, and prepare to leave for work.

Work and Learning

I take my breakfast at the workplace. During the day, we learn new things or continue working on our assigned tasks. This helps me gain more knowledge and improve my skills.

Evening Activities

We usually close from work at 3:00 p.m. or 3:30 p.m. When I get home, I pray and prepare my evening meal. After eating, I pray again and then go to bed to rest for the next day.
